Who is KVRI

At KV Rao International Foundation, we are dedicated to transforming rural India by fostering sustainable development and empowering communities. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by addressing key areas that shape a brighter future - education, sanitation, healthcare, agriculture, and farmer empowerment.

We believe that lasting change begins at the grassroots level. By providing children with access to quality education, improving healthcare services, ensuring clean water and sanitation, and supporting farmers with innovative agricultural practices, we are paving the way for self-reliant and thriving communities.

With the support of our partners, volunteers, and donors, we are committed to building a future where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to lead a healthy, dignified, and prosperous life. Join us in our mission to create a more equitable and sustainable rural India.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We focus on long-term, impactful solutions that create self-sufficient communities and preserve resources for future generations.

Empowerment

We equip individuals—especially farmers, women, and children—with knowledge, skills, and tools to shape their own futures.

Integrity

Transparency, accountability, and ethical practices are at the heart of everything we do, ensuring trust between us and the communities we serve.

Collaboration

We work hand in hand with local communities, volunteers, organizations, and donors, fostering partnerships that drive real and lasting change.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and sustainable technologies to solve complex challenges in education, healthcare, sanitation, and agriculture.

Equity & Inclusion

We ensure everyone, regardless of background, has access to the resources and opportunities needed to thrive.
